Refund Policy
1. Cases of Refund
Refund Policy for Doctor Delays:
If the doctor is late by 5 minutes or less, no deduction will be made from the
doctor's revenue.
If the doctor is late by 10 minutes, a 60% deduction will be made from the
doctor's revenue.
The deducted amount, if applicable, will be fully refunded to the patient in all cases.
Patient Cancellation:
Full refund if cancellation occurs more than 24 hours before the appointment.
60% refund if cancellation occurs between 24 and 18 hours before the
appointment.
No refund for cancellations made less than 18 hours before the appointment.
Doctor Cancellation:
If the doctor cancels less than 24 hours before the appointment, the patient is
refunded the full amount and receives a free session of equivalent value.
Additionally, the doctor provides one free session to another patient. The same
rule applies if the doctor fails to join the session.
If the doctor cancels more than 24 hours before the appointment, the patient
is refunded the full amount only.
2. Network Connection Issues:
No refunds will be issued in the event of network connection issues caused by
either the doctor or the patient.
3. Rescheduling Appointments:
Appointments cannot be rescheduled if the request is made within 18 hours of
the scheduled time.
Patients can reschedule if the request is made more than 18 hours before the
scheduled time.
4. Package Cancellations:
Calculation of Refunds:
The cost of each session is calculated as: Session Cost = Package Price /
Number of Sessions in the Package
Refunds follow the pro-rated method.
Before the Start of the Package:
If the first session is scheduled to start more than 24 hours from the
cancellation request, the full package amount is refunded.
If the first session is scheduled to start within 24 to 18 hours of the cancellation
request, 60% of the first sessions price is refunded along with the full price of
the remaining sessions.
After the Start of the Package:
Refunds will exclude the cost of any sessions that have already been
completed.
If the upcoming session is scheduled to start more than 24 hours from the
cancellation request, its full amount is refunded along with the full price of the
remaining sessions in the package.
If the upcoming session is scheduled to start within 24 to 18 hours of the
cancellation request, 60% of its price is refunded along with the full price of the
remaining sessions in the package.
